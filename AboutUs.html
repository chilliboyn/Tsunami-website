Our Story

Tsunami Security was founded in 2018 by Mr. Chilliboy Nkgudi, who initially launched the company under the name CCTV Master. With a background in IT and a deep understanding of technology, Mr. Nkgudi specialized in CCTV installation, providing top-notch surveillance solutions to homes and businesses. Recognizing the growing demand for comprehensive security services, we evolved from our roots as CCTV Master into Tsunami Security, broadening our offerings to include a range of advanced security solutions.
